Tufted Titmouse

How are they different?

<-- White-Breasted Nuthatches have a white throat, a darker, blue-gray back, and a longer beak.

Black capped chickadees have a black cap and throat, and a light gray back. -->

How are they different?

<-- Black-Capped Chickadees have a black cap and throat, and a more whitish-gray back.

Tufted Titmice have a gray head and back, a (sometimes seen) tuft on their head, a small black patch over their beak, and a light brown patch under their wings -->

White-Throated Sparrow
Song Sparrow
House Sparrow

How are they different?

<-- Female House Sparrows (males look less similar) have a yellow beak, a loosely patchy back, and white belly.

White-Throated Sparrows have a whiteish brown belly, a white throat (not pictured), yellow patches above the beak, and a striped, patchy brown back. -->

How are they different?

<-- White Throated Sparrows have a yellow patch above the beak, a more speckled back, with shades of white, brown, and black, and a whiteish brown belly.

Song Sparrows have a light brown-striped back, a striped belly, and brown wings. -->
