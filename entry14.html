As with all my recent projects, this is a bird project. This time, I’m taking my random bird photo generator and making it into a guessing game. Using the same photos (I might add more), I want to have it so when you click on the screen, it shows a photo of a bird and when you click again, it shows the name of the bird in the photo. So far I have it so when you click on the screen it shows a bird and it’s name, but I need to figure out how to separate the name and the photo. I think it’ll use an if else statement and a counter variable, and although Molly showed me how to do it, I need a refresher, so that will be my next step. I can’t wait to see how this project turns out!
