Hi! My name is Ellie. Ever since I saw a Mallard in Harlem Meer, Central Park at age 3, I've been obsessed with birds. As I grew older, my interest broadened from ducks to the songbirds I saw at a bird feeder I put up, to all birds in general. Over quarantine, I learned how to take photos of birds with a Sony camera, and have enjoyed trying to get fun photos of birds.

I coded this website for a computer science class, and have been able to make many bird projects exploring my passion for birds. All the pictures of birds on this website are photos I've taken. I hope you enjoy my website!
